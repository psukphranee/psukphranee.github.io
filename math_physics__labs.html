These are links to some Physics labs I've done at Cal Poly Pomona's Physics Department. In addition to these labs, I went through the common lower division Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs required for science majors. Doing these lab experiments involved the use of different instruments and involved varying amounts of research. A reoccuring instrument was the oscilloscope. These labs experiments ranged in difficulty from knowing exactly what was going on to knowing nothing.

The simpler experiments involved fairly basic concepts, such as prediciting capacitance from geometry and comparing them to empircal values. The more difficult ones required more research time into the topic because there were many pieces of information that needed to be pieced together. I spend a lot of time researching the experiments to get a firm understanding and to be able to explain it in my words. In addition to the science, I got better at LaTeXing.
