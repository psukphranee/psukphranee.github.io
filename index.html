About Me

 I earned my Bachelors in Math and Physics at Cal Poly Pomona and am currently the lead instructor at the Mathnasium of Woodland Hills. I worked as a math tutor at an afterschool program at Crenshaw HS and have a high success rate of improving students' performance. I work with kids ranging from 1st grade up to the 12th grade, covering Basic Math up to Calculus I. My instruction style is direct and conceptual, where I focus on building up the thought process and motivations leading up to the topic of discussion. My experience in math includes applying it to Physics, Machine Learning, and Robotics.
